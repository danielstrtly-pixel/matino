## üî≤ Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Orange background, white text, rounded-full (pill shape)
- **Secondary:** White background, charcoal text, subtle border
- **Ghost:** Transparent, text only

### Cards
- Background: Cream Light (`#FDFBF7`)
- Border: 1px Cream Dark (`#EDE8E0`)
- Border radius: 16px (rounded-2xl)
- Shadow: Soft, warm tinted

### Badges
- Rounded-full (pill shape)
- Small padding
- Colored backgrounds matching semantic meaning

## üìê Spacing
- Base unit: 4px
- Common: 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64px

## üåü Design Principles
1. **Warm & Inviting** - Use cream backgrounds, not stark white
2. **Fresh & Healthy** - Green accents for food/health related
3. **Action-Oriented** - Orange for CTAs to draw attention
4. **Organic Feel** - Rounded corners, soft shadows
5. **Clear Hierarchy** - Bold headings, readable body text
